What We've Done

We work with companies across all industries who are intersted in bringing intersectional thinking into their design process

the blackOUT blockparty

We teamed up with the blackOUT party up to present the first annual blackOUT block party. While carefully examining their exisiting resources and target-audience we helped to prepare both a in-person covid-safe experience and a virtaul show.

QuickSip

QuickSip is an innovative and CIC award winning mobile application that simplies the ordering process in bars. Researched and designed to enhance drink ordering, it also takes a careful look at how to maximize profits and tips for lower-level employees such as bartenders and servers.

African American PhDs in Computer Science Listserv

Along with Morehouse College, we explored the effictiveness of the only online space dedicated to African-American phD students, a listserv. Our qualitative findings led to several insights and recommendations aimed to improve the effects it has on computing identity as a marginalized person
