QuickSip

Problem Space

Waiters and bartenders earn almost 35K less than the national average. Yet, they make up 2.6 million jobs often working 12-hour shifts with minimal breaks. For waiters, it can be stressful to manage and keep track of customer orders especially during peak hours. This often results in poor customer service and bad reviews for the establishments. Additionally, customers face long and inconvenient wait times either standing in line or weaving through the crowd.

Solution

Waiters are often flooded with orders that can be difficult to track and manage. We streamlined the ordering process by design a mobile application that allows real-time ordering functions that appear on a shared iPad for bartenders. The mobile applications also enforces automated tips ensuring waiters are being accurately compensated.

In additon, the application offers automated user age verification, detailed view of the menus, order tracking features, and aimed to reduce wait time.
